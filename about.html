About Us

Easley Lawn and Garden, born in 1980. We've moved twice due to growth and are busting at the seams again. We have grown along with our wonderful community. Family and veteran-owned and operated, we exist to help in any way possible with your outdoor power needs. Easley Lawn and Garden was actually started before 1980 under a different name. We are the longest-standing full service outdoor power equipment dealership in Easley and the surrounding area. Our guiding principal is to do our absolute best to help you choose the ideal tool for your needs and budget. Then after the sale, to support you by supporting your equipment. Our dealership has a rock-solid reputation of providing excellent service, support, and satisfaction. We pride ourselves on offering our customers the best service possible, the parts and accessories you need, and a sincere, helpful staff that understands all aspects of our business. We have thousands of individual, recurring customers, and now we welcome you to the ELG family. Many thanks from all of us at Easley Lawn and Garden.
